# Aatrox matchups

How Aatrox fares in lane and when to pick him.

**Favorable**

- Immobile melees (Darius, Garen) — you can land Q sweet spots and kite with E.
- Short-range tops that rely on all-ins — your sustain and R reset outlast them.

**Skill matchups**

- Fiora, Riven — respect their burst and cooldowns; short trades, then all-in when key abilities are down.

**Tough**

- Ranged poke (Teemo, Quinn) — they harass from distance; play for jungle help or farm and scale.
