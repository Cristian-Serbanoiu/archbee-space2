# Aatrox synergies

Champions and comps that work well with Aatrox.

**Jungle**

- Engage junglers (Sejuani, Zac) set up your Q; you follow with damage and R resets.
- Carry junglers benefit from your CC and frontline in mid-game.

**Team comp**

- Aatrox likes follow-up CC and someone to absorb initial focus so he can land Qs and heal.
- Enchanters (Lulu, Soraka) amplify your healing; avoid stacking too many melees.

**Win conditions**

- Front-to-back with a strong frontline, or flank + R when the enemy is committed.
