# Aatrox Abilities

Detailed breakdown of Aatrox's kit.

## Passive — Deathbringer Stance

Periodically, Aatrox's next basic attack deals bonus physical damage and heals him. Hitting enemy champions with abilities or basic attacks reduces the cooldown.

## Q — The Darkin Blade

Aatrox slams his blade three times in a line. **Sweet spots** (blade edge) deal increased damage and knock up. Core ability for trading and all-in combos.

## W — Infernal Chains

Throws a chain that slows. If the target does not leave the zone, they are dragged to the center and briefly stunned.

## E — Umbral Dash

Short dash. During **World Ender**, grants bonus healing from all damage dealt.

## R — World Ender

Unleashes the Darkin form: gains movement speed, increased self-healing, and revives on takedown (with a short channel). Resets on champion takedown.

## Quick combo

Q1 → E (reposition for sweet spot) → W → Q2 → Q3. Use R before or during the engage for healing and resets.
