# Aatrox counters

Champions and tools that make life hard for the Darkin Blade.

**Lane counters**

- **Fiora** — Riposte blocks Q knockup and burst; she out-duels if ahead.
- **Ranged tops** — Teemo, Quinn, Vayne can space you and deny sweet spots.
- **Grievous Wounds** — cuts your healing; expect Executioner's or Thornmail.

**In teamfights**

- Heavy CC and burst can delete you before you get R resets.
- Kiting comps make it hard to land Q; wait for engage or flank.

For better pairings see **Aatrox synergies**; for lane logic see **Aatrox matchups**.
