# Aatrox lore

Aatrox is a **Darkin**: an ancient, corrupted Shuriman Ascended trapped inside his own weapon. He seeks to destroy all life so that his imprisonment might finally end.

**Key points**

- Once a noble warrior, he was twisted by the Void and sealed inside his blade.
- Whoever wields the blade is slowly consumed and body-jacked by Aatrox.
- His in-game goal is to end the world; he sees death as the only freedom.
